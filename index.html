Physics I Grading Rubric

Grade Types

P - Proficient ⓘ
Well executed, logically correct solution; has an overall logically sound argument (which may be required to be articulated in words). The solution may not be optimal, but it is correct physically, logically, and mathematically (barring very minor errors).
G - Getting There ⓘ
Exhibits solid problem-solving; more than half of the pieces of the solution are in place; you don't get all the way there but make significant progress toward a complete solution.
N - Needs Improvement ⓘ
Fails to connect many pieces; has less than half of logical flow of the solution demonstrates awareness of basics but not much more (writing only formulas does not count); exhibits at least some physical problem-solving competence.
L/B - Lost/Blank ⓘ
Throwing darts, nothing logical, does not demonstrate any comprehension of the concepts. Should have been (or was) left blank.

Boost Systems

Homework Boost

  • ≥90%: Replace MP with P
    OR single-level IP boost
  • ≥75%: Replace MP with G
  • ≥50%: Replace MP with N
  • Cannot replace L grades
  • Boosts always applied to help you optimally

Recitation Boosts

  • 8-9 attendances: 1 boost total
  • 10+ attendances: 2 boosts total
  • MP: 1 boost needed per level
    (N → G, G → P)
  • IP: 2 boosts needed per level
    (N → G, G → P)
  • Boosts always applied to help you optimally
